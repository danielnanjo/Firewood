About Us

Our story, values, and commitment to quality

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Firewood & More began as a small family operation dedicated to providing high-quality firewood to local communities. What started as a passion for sustainable forestry and woodcraft has grown into a thriving business that now includes handcrafted furniture and fireplace accessories.

Today, we continue to honor our roots by maintaining the same commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction that inspired our founders. Every piece of wood we sell is carefully selected and processed to ensure the best possible experience for our customers.

Our Mission

To provide premium wood products that enhance homes and lifestyles while promoting sustainable forestry practices and supporting local communities.

Our Values

Sustainability

We source wood responsibly, ensuring forests remain healthy for future generations.

Quality Craftsmanship

Every product is crafted with attention to detail and built to last.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction is our top priority from selection to delivery.

Community

We support local initiatives and employ local craftsmen.
